About Me Gallery Contact Me Artist Statement

My name is Melvin Kenneth Maduro Mingoa, a Filipino first-generation college student, born in the Philippines and raised in San Francisco.

I have always explored the inner workings of my mind and experimented with different artistic concepts and methods. I have many experiences exploring and coordinating in nature and use that as an inspiration to what I paint, draw, or conceptualize. Starting with my first transition into painting, then sculpture and photography, this website fully codded by myself, represents my journey to further allow a larger toolset to express myself and reflect the way I look at things. I am especially focused on the perspective relativity on many topics and the internal bias that I or others may have. I desire to show messages and stories through art that can be shown in a new way or in a way I find unique.
